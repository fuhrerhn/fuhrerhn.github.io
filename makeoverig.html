About Digistar Club

Indonesia's Next Digital Talent. Digistar Club is an inclusive community by Telkom Indonesia, specifically designed as a collaborative hub for young talents. We believe that every student possesses immense potential to become a driving force in Indonesia’s digital economy.

Here, we bridge the gap between academia and industry demands through a supportive ecosystem, providing access to professional mentors and real-world opportunities to contribute to the nation.

We offer an inclusive ecosystem for students across Indonesia to accelerate their digital potential through direct access to Telkom Group’s professional mentors. By combining an industry-relevant curriculum with hands-on experience, Digistar Club provides exclusive career exposure and the essential skills needed to thrive in the national digital landscape.
